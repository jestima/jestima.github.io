Short Bio: Jacinto Estima is an Assistant Professor at the Technology Department of “Universidade Europeia”, Portugal, and a researcher at the Information and Decision Support Systems Lab of INESC-ID, where he works on problems related to the Geographical Information Sciences and Information Management, more specifically on using machine learning to extract relevant information from VGI data and social media. He is also an invited Assistant Professor at the School of Technology of Setúbal - Polytechnic Institute of Setúbal.
